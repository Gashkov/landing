ONLY THINK IN RIDING

Are you ready to live this adventure?

Because our main goal is that cyclists just think about the most important thing, which is the competition itself. They just have to concentrate on pedaling and enjoy the TRANSPYR 2017 to the fullest.

The equipment that we offer you in the seventh edition of TRASNPYR has a long experience in the world of mountain biking competitions. Our team consists of physiotherapists, mechanics, trainers and logistics personnel to provide the best service.

Advice and stage by stage approach.
Massages pre and post stage by physiotherapists.

MECHANICS TRANSPYR 2017

ASSEMBLY OF BIKE AND SETTING IT UP FOR THE RACE. The first day in the same camp, once you have given us the bike in its carrying case. ASSISTANCE DURING THE RACE Every day at the end of the stage, the riders leave the bike
at our mechanic's tent where we clean, check, grease and repair it.
Once we are done, we leave the bike hanging in place. TRANSFER BETWEEN STAGES We take care of packing the bike for this day. LAST DAY At the end of the race we put the bike back in its carrying case so that it can be sent back home. All work is included, the material will be charged separately. MASSAGE AND PHISIOTHERAPY TRANSPYR 2017

The function and purpose of this service is to reduce muscle overcharge, contractures that may occur during race days, as well as treat any injuries that may arise. We work with intensity friction techniques, moderate massage, palmar pressures, vibration techniques, stretching, etc. A leg massage the day before the first stage, post-competition massage after each stage and special care of injuries are included. Materials are also included in the price. Every day, once the stage is finished, we will take care of scheduling the massage.
320 €
